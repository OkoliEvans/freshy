    let's transform food service together.

    The world has always been changed by visionaries and action takers, and innovation has always been a big feat achieved only by the truly dedicated. We like to think that we have both the vision and the dedication to make remarkable change to our community, and in the food service industry... and this is where we need you.

    Freshy is a disruptive food service app that connects food vendors to their customers. We provide a central point, a kind of market where your customers can easily place orders for your dishes and have them delivered to their locations. Our job is to connect you to your fans and customers, but we will also help you find many more customers through our wide coverage and marketing campaigns.
